Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to be enrolled at Connect Clinic?

No. Connect Clinic is a telehealth service — you don't need to leave your current GP. We see patients for specific needs like repeat prescriptions, medical certificates, or assessments. For ongoing care we recommend staying enrolled with a regular practice.

How quickly can I get an appointment?

We aim to confirm appointments within a few hours of your booking request. Same-day consultations are often available. We'll contact you by email or phone to confirm your time.

Is this covered by ACC or a Community Services Card?

Connect Clinic is currently a private pay service and is not subsidised. Our fees are listed upfront so there are no surprises. ACC claims for injury-related consults may be available — ask us when you book.

What do I need for the video call?

Just your phone, tablet, or computer with a camera and internet connection. We'll send you a link before your appointment — no app download required. Just click and you're in.

Can you prescribe medications?

Yes. As a fully registered NZ GP, Dr Katoa can prescribe most medications. Prescriptions are sent electronically directly to your chosen pharmacy. Some controlled substances may require an in-person visit.

Is my information kept private?

Absolutely. All consultations are confidential and managed in accordance with the NZ Health Information Privacy Code. Your records are stored securely and not shared without your consent.
