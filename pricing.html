Things worth knowing

  • All prices are in New Zealand dollars and include GST where applicable.
  • Payment is taken in full at booking through Stripe. We never store card details.
  • If a consult is outside what we can safely cover by video, we will tell you in the first three minutes and refund the consult.
  • We do not see patients outside Aotearoa New Zealand. We do not see under-18s. For under-18s, please book through your usual in-person GP.
  • Connect Clinic is not a crisis service. If you or someone you love is in mental health crisis, call or text 1737, or 111 if there is risk of harm right now.
  • Funding for Connect Clinic apps (Talanoa, Mahino, GLP-1 Companion, Pasifika Medication Resource) sits separately and never depends on consult fees.
