## 5. Engagement Strategy

### Best Times to Post for NZ Audience

Based on NZ audience behaviour patterns:

| Time slot | Why it works |
|---|---|
| **Tuesday-Thursday, 7am-9am** | Before-work scroll. People check phones before the day starts. |
| **Tuesday-Thursday, 12pm-1pm** | Lunch break browsing. High engagement window. |
| **Sunday, 7pm-9pm** | Weekend wind-down. People are relaxed and receptive. |

Avoid: Monday mornings (too busy), Friday afternoons (everyone's checked out), Saturday mornings (family time).

Start with 3 posts per week. Once you have 4-6 weeks of data, check Instagram Insights to see when your specific audience is most active — then adjust to match.

### Using the Link in Bio to Drive Bookings

A single link in an Instagram bio is prime real estate. Here's how to use it well:

**Option A — Direct to booking page (simplest)**
Link straight to the Connect Clinic website booking page. Works fine if the site has a clear booking flow.

**Option B — Linktree (recommended)**
Set up a free Linktree (linktree.com) with buttons for:
- Book an appointment (main CTA — top of the list)
- Services and fees
- Wegovy / weight management info
- Patient guide
- Email us

This way, followers from different posts can find what they're looking for without digging through the site.

**In captions:** Always end with "Book at the link in bio." This phrase trains followers to know where to go. Keep it consistent across all posts.

**In Stories:** Use the link sticker (the chain icon) to link directly from stories. Stories drive higher click-through than feed posts. Every story with a CTA should have a link sticker.

---

### Content Maintenance Notes

- **Respond to comments** within 24 hours. Even a simple "Thanks for your question!" helps the algorithm and builds trust.
- **Share patient wins (de-identified)** in Stories — e.g. "Had someone sort their prescription in 10 minutes today. That's the goal."
- **Repost relevant NZ health news** to Stories with a brief comment. This keeps the page active without requiring original content every day.
- **Check Insights monthly** and note which posts had the best reach, saves, and profile visits. Double down on what works.
- **Month 2+:** Consider Instagram Reels — even a 30-second talking-head video from Dr Katoa gets far more reach than a static post. Low production value is fine. Authenticity outperforms polish every time.
